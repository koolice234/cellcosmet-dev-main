Shopify Email Templates

Author: Pat McNamara

These emails use foundation for emails and can be easily edited using scss. There is no need to copy and paste new styles into every email because foundation takes care of that. All it takes is updating styles, previewing in the browser and then pasting each email into the email editor in shopify.
